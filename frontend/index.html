Blockchain Course Project

AI image provenance through watermarking and blockchain.

This academic prototype embeds an invisible LSB watermark, creates a SHA-256 fingerprint, and registers verification metadata on a local Ethereum-compatible blockchain.

On-chain provenance

The hash, watermark, creator address, and timestamp are recorded by a smart contract.

Interpretation note: “Authentic” means the file is registered and byte-identical to the official version. It does not prove that the depicted event is true or that the image was generated by AI.
